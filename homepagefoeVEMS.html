ABOUT THE APP

Key features of our app

Purpose

VEMS is a Volunteer Event Management System that verifies the participation of individuals at events they have signed up for by organizing their engagement hours.

Solution

With the way volunteer hours are tracked now, it is very easy for a volunteer to falsify the times they spent at a specific event and just get it signed by a parent/guardian to meet certain requirements.

Additionally, volunteer agencies have trouble keeping track of the specific hours of each of their volunteers. VEMS is an event manager that aims to solve these problems by organizing the amount of hours that a volunteer spends at each event.

Effortless Service

As easy and familiar as scanning a flight boarding pass, VEMS provides a QR code to each volunteer at the time they sign up for a specific event. When the volunteer shows up at the event, their QR code will be shown to the QR code reader which scans their volunteer information, stores it in the database, and completes the check-in.

Benefit

If the volunteer check-in process is automated at the event site, individuals' hours won't go missing and administrators/organizers would be able to know with a surety that volunteers actually participate in the events that they put on their volunteer logs. With VEMS, one can ultimately make the check-in process a lot faster, which leaves more time for what's important: Volunteering!
